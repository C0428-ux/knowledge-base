## Executive Summary

An action plan for **democratizing AI-powered cyber defense**.

> *"Artificial intelligence is reshaping cybersecurity. The same capabilities that help defenders identify vulnerabilities, automate remediation, and respond faster are also being used by malicious actors to scale attacks, lower barriers to entry, and increase sophistication."*

---

## The Challenge

The United States and its allies face a **rapidly changing cyber threat environment**, and private-sector innovators have an important responsibility to help meet that challenge.

### Key Context
- AI capabilities benefit both defenders AND malicious actors
- Attackers use AI to scale attacks, lower barriers to entry, and increase sophistication
- Defenders use AI to identify vulnerabilities, automate remediation, and respond faster

---

## The Action Plan: Five Pillars

The plan, informed by conversations with cybersecurity and national security experts across **federal and state government and major commercial entities**, consists of five pillars:

1. **Democratizing cyber defense**
2. **Coordinating across government and industry**
3. **Strengthening security around frontier cyber capabilities**
4. **Preserving visibility and control in deployment**
5. **Enabling users to protect themselves**

---

## Core Vision

> *"Building resilience in the Intelligence Age will require both working through democratic institutions and processes, and broadening access to the technologies that can help protect communities, critical systems, and our national security."*

OpenAI commits to:
- Building infrastructure to support cybersecurity defenders
- Organizing around democratizing access to defensive tools
- Making trusted defensive capabilities available across society
