# Google Gemini Updates to Docs, Sheets, Slides and Drive

**Source:** Google Blog (March 10, 2026)  
**Availability:** Google AI Ultra and Pro subscribers

---

## Overview

Google is enhancing Gemini AI integration across Docs, Sheets, Slides, and Drive. These tools now help users write documents, create spreadsheets, design presentations, and find information across files and emails.

> "When you select your sources, Gemini can now pull relevant information from your files, emails and the web to securely connect dots and uncover useful insights, while keeping your information safeguarded."

**Availability:** Beta rollout started March 10, 2026 for Google AI Ultra and Pro subscribers.

---

## Gemini in Docs

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Create from sources** | Generate new documents from scratch using relevant information from files and emails (e.g., "turning last season's notes into a ready-to-use coaching plan") |
| **Refine writing** | Unify voice and style across documents (e.g., "change the tone...to feel more warm and welcoming") |
| **Match doc format** | Automatically align to a reference document's style (e.g., "pull all the details for your upcoming trip to Nashville and have it match your favorite itinerary template") |

---

## Gemini in Sheets

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Build from prompts** | Create custom, visual spreadsheets from a single prompt (e.g., "personal finance tracker that organizes your data around your specific goals") |
| **Fill with Gemini** | Auto-populate tables with summarized, categorized, or brand-new data from existing sheets or the web (e.g., "label your financial data to more easily track your income and expenses") |
| **Visual transformation** | Convert simple prompts into visual spreadsheets (e.g., "personalized health tracker that aligns your workouts and nutrition with your overall health goals") |

> 📊 **Note:** Data based on a 95-participant study comparing manual entry against Fill with Gemini on a 100-cell task.

---

## Gemini in Slides

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Draft from prompt** | Generate new slides with a single prompt (e.g., "persuasive pitch for your next family getaway") |
| **Draft from information** | Create slides based on provided info (e.g., "life update" slide) |
| **Edit slide tone** | Modify slides to match vision (e.g., "dialing up the playful tone") |
| **Notes to presentation** | Convert rough notes to ready-to-present slides while you focus on the vision |

Gemini generates "professional layouts, fully editable diagrams and entire presentations from scratch — handling the design and formatting so you can stay focused on your story."

---

## Gemini in Drive

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Find files & answer questions** | Locate files and synthesize complex information (e.g., "synthesizing your research on sleep benefits") |
| **AI Overviews** | Surface relevant files (e.g., "simplify your tax prep by letting Gemini surface recent document downloads and last year's return") |
| **Ask Gemini** | Compare documents and highlight key differences (e.g., "compare catering proposals...highlighting cost differences and key contract clauses") |

> ⚠️ **Note:** Drive features are currently **U.S. only** at launch.
