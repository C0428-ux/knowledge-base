# Your Car with Google Built-in Is About to Get Smarter, Thanks to Gemini

**Source:** Google Blog - April 30, 2026
**URL:** https://blog.google/products-and-platforms/platforms/android/cars-with-google-built-in-gemini-tips-2026/
**分类:** AI产品发布

---

## Overview

Gemini is replacing Google Assistant in cars with Google built-in, offering a more natural, conversational way to manage navigation, music, and vehicle settings. This update is rolling out to both new and existing vehicles, starting with English-language users in the United States.

---

## Key Features

### Natural Conversation
- **No rigid voice commands** — speak naturally to Gemini, hands-free
- Manage navigation, music, messages, and car settings through free-flowing conversation

### Gemini Live (Beta)
- Opens new ways to **learn and brainstorm while driving**
- Tap the Gemini Live button from the overlay or say **"Hey Google, let's talk"** to start

### Vehicle-Specific Intel
- Gemini **integrates with your car's owner's manual** to answer questions about unique features
- Deep integration with vehicle systems through close collaboration with automakers

### Future Expansion
- More languages and countries coming
- Ability to safely access **Gmail, Calendar, and Google Home** apps

---

## Availability & Access

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Rollout** | Starting with English in the United States |
| **Eligibility** | Both new and existing vehicles with Google built-in |
| **Update Type** | Software rollout |

### How to Upgrade
1. Sign into your Google Account in your car
2. Watch for an upgrade prompt on your dashboard

### How to Use Gemini Once Upgraded
- Say **"Hey Google"**
- Tap the microphone on the home screen
- Use your **steering wheel button**

## Context

Google built-in launched in 2020 with the promise that vehicles would receive ongoing improvements. Gemini represents the next evolution of this commitment, bringing AI-powered assistance to both new and existing vehicles through over-the-air software updates.
