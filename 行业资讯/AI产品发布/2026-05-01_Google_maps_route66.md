# Check out the new ways to explore Route 66 on Google Maps

**Source:** Google Blog - April 30, 2026
**URL:** https://blog.google/products-and-platforms/products/maps/route-66/
**分类:** AI产品发布

---

## Overview

Route 66 is one of the most iconic highways in the U.S., stretching 2,448 miles from Chicago to Santa Monica. In celebration of its 100th anniversary, Google is rolling out two new ways to help you explore it, virtually or IRL.

---

## New Features

### Cruise the Mother Road from your couch

For the first time, when you search for **"U.S. Route 66" on Maps**, you'll see the entire route mapped out on your Android or iOS device. You can also use Street View to see landmarks — including Elmer's Bottle Tree Ranch in California and Cadillac Ranch in Texas — up close.

### Map out your must-see stops

If you'll be driving Route 66, check out our new **shared list** to find both well-known landmarks and hidden gems — in addition to the best local restaurants along the way.
