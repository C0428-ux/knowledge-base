## Overview

Stargate is OpenAI's long-term initiative to build the compute foundation required to deliver AGI benefits broadly and reliably worldwide. The program aims to meet accelerating AI demand from consumers, businesses, developers, and governments.

---

## Progress & Milestones

> "When we announced Stargate in January 2025, we committed to securing 10GW of AI infrastructure in the United States by 2029. Just over a year later, we have already surpassed that milestone, with more than 3GW added in the last 90 days alone."

**Key Metrics:**
- **10GW** original commitment by 2029 — **achieved early**
- **3GW** added in the past 90 days
- Demand continues to accelerate

## Strategic Approach

### Why More Compute?
- Enables better model training
- Serves models reliably
- Improves performance
- Lowers costs over time
- Brings powerful tools to more people

### The AI Flywheel

```
More compute → Better models → More usage → 
Improved products/revenue → Reinvest in infrastructure
```

## Abilene, Texas Case Study

The Stargate site in Abilene demonstrates:
- Fast, responsible construction
- Community partnership
- Growth and stewardship combined

### Water Usage Details

> "Like many data centers, the Abilene site uses closed-loop cooling rather than traditional evaporative cooling towers. Once the system is filled, water continuously moves through sealed pipes and is recirculated rather than consumed."

| Phase | Water Usage |
|-------|-------------|
| Initial fill (per building) | ~2 Olympic-sized swimming pools |
| Annual use at full buildout | Comparable to a medium-sized office building (~4 average households) |
